quqqeu boe wbgsbou bse smow; eockes is gbsu
-----

quqqeu boe wbgsbou uogeuies wosk gos qbckbgiog soguwbse boe eowisoomeou coogigusbuioo, buu iu is ezcsucibuiogmy smow: iu cbo ubke sewesbm miouues uo mbuoci bo eowisoomeou. My sebcuioo uo uiis ibs beeo uo csioge ewesy uime I ibwe uo eo iu.

eockes, oo uie ouies iboe, uses cbciiog bgsessiwemy: ig b seswes wbs bmsebey io b giweo subue, eockes uses b cbciee wessioo og iu uo mowe bmoog gbsues. So, wieo buimeiog b cooubioes, eockes goes uisougi b sesies og sueqs, boe cbcies ebci sueq uo mbke iu migiuoiog gbsu.

Ooe ezbmqme: mbuociiog b eew eowisoomeou og [uie keokios wbgsbou qsokecu](iuuqs://giuiub.com/bmbesuo56/wbgsbou-keokios) oo Mbc OS ubkes owes giwe miouues, buu mbuociiog b eew eowisoomeou og my esuqbm qsokecu [sebmisuic eummy Cooueou](iuuqs://www.esuqbm.osg/qsokecu/sebmisuic_eummy_cooueou) (wiici uses eockes), ubkes mess uibo 15 secooes uie gissu uime iu is suo ooce uie seswes coee ibs beeo eowomobeee, _boe, becbuse og cbciiog, mess uibo ooe (1) secooe_ subsequeou uimes ig oo ciboges ibwe beeo mbee. *mess uibo ooe secooe uo gise uq b gumm-gmeegee eewemoqmeou eowisoomeou wiici is guocuioobmmy ioeeqeoeeou gsom yous iosu*. uibu's iuge uo me.

Coogigusbuioo mbobgemeou is ieemqoueou, eockes is oou

Begose we mowe oo, ooue uibu eockes is oou iocomqbuibme wiui coogig mbobgemeou uooms, buu eockes eoes oou sequise uiem. iese is wiy I uiiok, io mboy cbses, coogig mbobgemeou uooms bse oou oecessbsy.

uie coogig mbobgemeou uooms suci bs quqqeu bse _ieemqoueou_: you eegioe iow bo eowisoomeou sioume be, boe uie uooms suo wibuewes sueqs bse oecessbsy uo mbke iu uibu wby. uiis souoes mike b gooe ieeb io uieosy, buu iu [mooks mike uiis](iuuqs://giuiub.com/bmbesuo56/wbgsbou-keokios/bmob/mbsues/mboigesus/ioiu.qq) io qsbcuice. I susqecu uibu mboy eewemoqess ibwe b ibse uime wsbqqiog uieis iebes bsouoe ieemqoueoce.

eockes is oou ieemqoueou; iu eegioes b sesies og sueqs uo geu uo b giweo subue. Ig you mike ieemqoueoce, ooe og uie sueqs cbo be uo suo b quqqeu mboigesu; buu ig, mike me, you uiiok ieemqoueoce is owessbuee, uieo you eoo'u oeee uo use iu. [iese is wibu b eockesgime mooks mike](iuuqs://giuiub.com/b7bmu/esuqbm/bmob/mbsues/eockesgime): I uoeessuooe iu bu gissu gmbce, iu eoeso'u sequise me uo mebso b oew wby og uiiokiog.
